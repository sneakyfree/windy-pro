Frequently Asked Questions:
Can I have too many models? Nope! They just take disk space. WindyTune juggles them for you like a pro circus performer. 🎪
Can I have too few? One model is all you need to get started. Think of it as your starter Pokémon — you can catch more later. 😄
Can I download more models later? Absolutely! Settings → Model Manager, anytime. Browse, download, delete. It's like Netflix for voice models.
Can I change my mind? Yes! Delete one, try another, mix and match. We won't judge. Experimentation is encouraged. 🔬
Can I select all 15 models? If your plan includes them — YES! Go full Pokédex. Gotta catch 'em all. WindyTune picks the best one moment by moment.
How do power users mix and match? Check out the Comparison Matrix below — click any row to select it. Your plan determines which models are available.
Do I have to switch models manually? Never ever. WindyTune reads your CPU, RAM, battery, even ambient noise. It's like having a personal DJ for your voice engine. Grandma-simple or power-user deep: your call. 🎧
